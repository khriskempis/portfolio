ABOUT

I am a musician and developer currently located in the Los Angeles area. I attended the Full Stack Flex program at Thinkful where I focused on building applications designed to help people.

My passion for learning only enhances my passion for teaching. When I am not coding, I am meeting with my piano students, practicing some sick bass lines, or playing my favorite board games. You can find me perfoming and gigging around town working local musicals.

Lets work together!

PROJECTS

Music Tests

Currently in beta, Music Tests is a web app designed to help music students practice thier sight-reading skills. It functions much like flash cards with features such as an interactive keyboard, timed tests, and tracking scores.

Music Tests was built using a React frontend with a Node backend.

Inventory Management

A simple way to track and manage items in a company's stock. This website was inspired by my brother who is a business owner and was in need of a way to organize and track his growing inventory.

Inventory Management was built using jQuery, Node, and MongoDB

Data Breach

Ever wonder why you get all that spam email? Data Breach answers that question and let's you know what company suffered a hack where your email may have gotten stolen.

Data Breach was built using jQuery and the API offered by haveibeenpwned.com
