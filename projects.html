Music Tests

Currently in beta, Music Tests is a web app designed to help music students practice their sight-reading skills. It functions much like flash cards with features such as an interactive keyboard, timed tests, and tracking scores.

Music Tests was built using a React frontend with a Node backend.

A simple way to track and manage items in a company's stock. This website was inspired by my brother who is a business owner and was in need of a way to organize and track his growing inventory.

Ever wonder why you get all that spam email? Data Breach answers that question and let's you know what company suffered a hack where your email may have gotten stolen.

Data Breach was built using jQuery and the API offered by haveibeenpwned.com
